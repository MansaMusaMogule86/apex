## ADDITIONAL DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

### Animations & Interactions
- Subtle page transitions (fade/slide)
- Counter animations for statistics
- Hover states on interactive elements
- Progress bar for scenario simulations
- Pulse effects on live data indicators

### Data Visualizations
- Clean, minimal charts (use gold/blue accent colors)
- Real estate market heat maps
- Growth trajectory curves
- Network/relationship graphs
- AI agent coordination diagrams

### Photography Direction
- Ultra-luxury real estate (Dubai penthouses, waterfront villas)
- Executive environments (modern offices, boardrooms)
- Technology imagery (subtle, not cliché)
- Abstract representations of intelligence/networks
- High contrast, dramatic lighting

### Typography Hierarchy
- **H1 (Slide Titles):** 48-64px, Cormorant Garamond, Light weight
- **H2 (Section Headers):** 32-40px, Cormorant Garamond
- **Body:** 16-18px, Outfit, Regular weight
- **Data/Stats:** 24-48px, DM Mono or Cormorant
- **Labels/Captions:** 12-14px, DM Mono, Uppercase, Tracking wide

### Iconography
- Use Lucide-style icons (thin stroke, 1.5px)
- Consistent icon set across all slides
- Gold or white icons on dark backgrounds
- Animated micro-interactions on key icons
